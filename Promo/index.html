How it works

Easy to pass this type of information to other people by voice (eg by phone) if it is not convenient to text it.

Place the mnemonic string instead of your contacts information on the billboards, newspapers, tv ads etc.

3 easy steps to do:

  1. Get a mnemonic string on one side
  2. Tell it your interlocutor
  3. Get original string on the other side
